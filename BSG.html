Projects In Development

Among The Hunted - You are being hunted by ruthless cold-blooded killers, your only objective
is to survive. Alone, hungry and confused you must survive this cruel sport using
only what you can find in this strange foreign land.

-Gameplay You must survive for a total of 24 real time hours to conquer the game.
Are you up for the task?

This harrowing experience is one you will not soon forget. Use the Island's treacherous landscape to your advantage and outsmart the vicious hunters, for they outnumber and outgun you.

Watch and listen as the enemy's AI catches on to your tactics and adapts accordingly. Feel the exhileration of being prey and do whatever you can to survive

At Baked Steak Games

We want Among The Hunted to be as strikingly human as possible. This means that this game won't be another shoot 'em up style game;
we want to encourage the fact that the hunters are stronger, faster, and better equipped than you. You will have to carefully plan
how to combat the hunters because they will kill you much easier than you can kill them.
